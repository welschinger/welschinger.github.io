About

I am broadly interested in how machines can learn to understand and predict the physical world. My background is in mathematics; my current work sits at the intersection of generative models, physical dynamics, and representation learning.

Research focus

World models

How can we build models that predict, not just interpolate? Object-centric and predictive representations of scene evolution.

Physical dynamics

Grounding generative video in motion and physical plausibility — beyond surface-level appearance.

Representation learning

Self-supervised and alignment-based objectives for video and visual foundation models.

Geometric deep learning

Learning on shapes and surfaces — PDE solvers, neural representations, and structure-aware models.
